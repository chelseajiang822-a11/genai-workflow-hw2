# GenAI Workflow: Business Email Risk Flagging and Rewriting

## Workflow

This project focuses on detecting potential risks in internal business emails and suggesting safer rewrites using an LLM.

## User

Employees or managers who draft internal or external business emails.

## Input

A draft email that may contain inappropriate tone, compliance risks, or unclear communication.

## Output

- A risk assessment (whether the email contains potential issues)
- The type of risk (e.g., tone, legal, compliance)
- A revised version of the email with safer and more professional wording

## Value

This task is valuable to automate because:

- It reduces communication risk in organizations
- It improves professionalism and consistency
- It helps prevent legal or reputational issues
